About

Vancouver, BC.
Usually outside.

Career

Startups, sales,
and making things work.

I've spent my career inside startups — from early-stage scrappiness to the messier middle of scaling. I started in sales, got good at it, and gradually found myself pulled toward the harder, more interesting problems: how do you actually build something that lasts?

Over time I've moved from closing deals to a more forward-deployed product role — sitting at the intersection of customers, product, and go-to-market. I like taking on the initiatives nobody has quite figured out yet, and turning them into something that works.

Outside work

When I'm not at a desk, I'm usually somewhere in British Columbia — or somewhere further afield — chasing the kind of day you don't forget. I ski, surf, and ride my bike, preferably somewhere new.

I also shoot photography. It started as a way to remember places and has turned into something I take seriously — I take on the occasional commercial project when the work is interesting.
